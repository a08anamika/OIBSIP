ABOUT US

Who we are

For years, our organizations across the globe, both large and small, trust our team to provide specialized services. Our breadth and depth of experience in the library community provides our clients a unique array of expertise, positioning us as the ultimate partner for institutions looking to preserve the historic, artistic, monetary and creative value of their library collections. Exceptional, efficient service is a standard we believe in.

We work with a range of institutions, both large and small.Our approach is collaborative. What we consider a standard process may need to be adapted to fit the nuances of your collection. We want to first understand what you’re trying to accomplish, so we can problem-solve together and sort out the options that best meet your needs.
